TikReelsSaver Privacy Policy

1. Intended Users

The App is intended for users aged 14 and above. If you are under the age of 14, please do not use the App. If we discover that users below the required age are using the App, we may take appropriate measures to restrict access.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not actively collect personally identifiable information. However, during use, you may provide or allow access to the following:

  • Provided Video Link Information: When you enter a video link, the App processes it to provide a download function but does not store the link or related content.
  • Storage Permission: The App may require access to your device storage to download and save videos, but it will not access other files.
  • Crash Log Data: If the App crashes, we may collect device-related information, such as device model, operating system version, and error logs. This data is solely used for analyzing issues and improving App performance and does not contain any personally identifiable information.

3. Use of Information

The App processes video downloads only on the local device and does not upload data to any server or share it with third parties. Your download history and video files are entirely managed by you.

Crash log data is only used internally for analysis and App improvements and will not be used for any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party APIs to retrieve video content. These services may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of relevant service providers to understand how your data is processed.

5. Information Security

We value your privacy and take reasonable measures to ensure your data security. The App does not store, collect, or share any personal information.

6. Compliance with Laws

The use of this App must comply with relevant laws and regulations and is intended for personal use only. Please ensure that any videos you download and share comply with the usage policies of the respective service platforms to avoid violating copyrights or other rights.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in laws or App functionality. Please check for the latest version regularly.
